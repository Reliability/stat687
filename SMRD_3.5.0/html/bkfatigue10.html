Yokobori fatigue-fracture test

Description

Yokobori~(1951) describes a fatigue-fracture test on .41% carbon steel cylindrical specimens, tested at \pm 37.1 kg/mm^2 stress amplitude. The data are given on page 224-225 of Bogdanoff and Kozin (1985).

Format

A data.frame with 63 rows and 1 variable:

[, 1] kilocycles Accumulated cycles at failure (in thousands) Numeric

Source

Bogdanoff J.L. and Kozin F. (1985) Probablistic Models of Cumulative Damage, New York: John Wiley & Sons.

Yokobori (1951)
