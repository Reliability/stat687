nicdbattery {SMRD}R Documentation

Accelerated test of spacecraft battery cell data

Description

charge-discharge cycles data truncated because of burn-in and removal of an unknown number of DOAs

Format

A data.frame with 87 rows and 10 variables:

[, 1] celsius Temperature applied to the battery Categoric
[, 2] discharge_depth Depth of discharge (as percent) Categoric
[, 3] discharge_time Discharge time (in hours) Categoric
[, 4] charge Charge time (in hours) Categoric
[, 5] recharge Level of recharge Categoric
[, 6] koh_percent Concentration of Potassium Hydroxide (as percent) Categoric
[, 7] koh_volume Volume of Potassium Hydroxide (in cubic-centimeters) Categoric
[, 8] precharge Precharge time (in hours) Categoric
[, 9] cycles Accumulated cycles at event Numeric
[,10] event Event observed at cycles (failure/right-censored) Categoric

Source

Brown and Mains (1979)
