adhesivebondc {SMRD}R Documentation

Adhesive bonded power element test

Description

How do pounds and days work together? Two responses? Constant strain?

Format

A data.frame with 336 rows and 4 variables:

[, 1] celsius Temperature applied to the bond Categoric
[, 2] rh Relative humidity applied to the bond Categoric
[, 3] days Time the bond was under load Categoric
[, 4] pounds Load at which the bond failed Numeric

Source

W.Q. Meeker and G. Hahn (1985), Statistical investigation of the fatigue life of deep-groove ball bearings, Journal of Research, National Bureau of Standards, 57, 273–316.
