Diesel generator fan failures

Description

These data are diesel generator fan failures. Failures in 12 of 70 generator fans were reported at times ranging between 450 hours and 8,750 hours. Of the 58 units that did not fail, the reported running times (i.e., censoring times) ranged between 460 and 11,500 hours. Different fans had different running times because units were introduced into service at different times and because their use-rates differed.

Format

A data.frame with 37 rows and 3 variables:

[, 1] hours Accumulated time at event Numeric
[, 2] event Event observed at hours (failure/right-censored) Categoric
[, 3] count Number of events observed at hours Numeric

Source

Nelson, W. (1982), Applied Life Data Analysis, pg 133, New York, John Wiley & Sons Inc.
